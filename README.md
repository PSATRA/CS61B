## Design Philosophy
1. Our final purpose is to design 
for users, regardless of what's 
happening under the hook.
That's why we design `sentinel`, 
AList with 100 boxes, 
fake indices, etc.


2. Learn to **actively analyze** and **optimize** your own data structure.
